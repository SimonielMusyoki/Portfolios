OUR STORY

OUR STORY

We will keep our story short and exciting! The word Engaruka represents the historical and natural archaeological remains, in the Monduli area, that locals used to construct terraces, ages ago, to distribute water to their fields, to support their economic activities.

As a young and energetic firm, we are always inspired to create lasting impact and impressions that will exist in years to come, highlighted by what our name represents. In light of this, we look forward to working with you, to enable you achieve your goals through our significant emphasis on professional, ethical and professional standards in our delivery.
